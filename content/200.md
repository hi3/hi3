## Huntington Woods at 200 Blakeslee Street

This page is a complete reference for deliveries at the 200 Blakeslee Street apartment complex.
Use it for locker-first workflow, building lookup, and exact unit location information.

## Quick Rules

- Start at the {{< google-maps-link q="41.672336,-72.929246" >}}apartment hub locker{{< /google-maps-link >}}, then deliver rejected packages door-to-door.
- Ground floor units are odd numbers.
- Second floor units are even numbers.
- Units wrap counter-clockwise from the center building sign.
- If a locker rejected package is still pinned at the locker, drive to the unit building, walk to the unit door and re-pin there.
- Follow Call/Text/Call procedure before marking undeliverable.

## One-Minute Workflow

1. Process all locker-eligible packages first.
2. Park safely at the {{< google-maps-link q="41.672336,-72.929246" >}}locker{{< /google-maps-link >}}
3. Gather all totes / overflow packages, and carry one empty tote for rejects.
4. Put every rejected package into the reject tote.
5. Once you complete a tote, move the rejects into the now empty tote.
6. Keep rejected packages grouped by original tote/color when possible.
7. Deliver packages rejected by the locker to apartment front doors.
8. Scan at door, photo at door, complete.

	9. If access is blocked, run Call/Text/Call before any undeliverable action.
	10. Repeat this sequence for each reject to stay fast and consistent.

## Locker Rejected Procedure

1. Assume GPS pin is often still at the locker.
2. Find building by unit range.
3. Drive to the building and locate apartment section.
4. Walk to the unit using front/back pair mapping.
5. If needed, use "GPS not working, I am at location."
6. Scan the package(s) at door, take photo(s) at door, complete.
7. If blocked, perform Call/Text/Call procedure before marking undeliverable.

## Section Labels

- `L`: Left
- `LC`: Left-Center (24-unit only)
- `CL`: Center-Left
- `C`: Center (20-unit only)
- `CR`: Center-Right
- `RC`: Right-Center (24-unit only)
- `R`: Right

Patterns:

- 8-unit: `L | R`
- 16-unit: `L | CL | CR | R`
- 20-unit: `L | CL | C | CR | R`
- 24-unit: `L | LC | CL | CR | RC | R`
